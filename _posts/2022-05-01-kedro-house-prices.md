## Introduction

[Project Repository](https://github.com/inigohidalgo/kedro-house-prices-kaggle)

In an effort to learn more about MLOps I am building an example pipeline using simple datasets which I am aiming to eventually include:

- Data ingestion
- Data processing
- Model training
- Serving
- Orchestrating
- Simple CI/CD pipeline

With the first four points partially or wholly handled within Kedro.
